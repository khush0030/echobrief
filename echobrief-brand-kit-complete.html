08 · Brand Voice

Voice & Tone

Warm, precise, inclusive, confident. Never jargony. Voice stays the same across both themes.

Warm
Like a trusted colleague — not a cold enterprise dashboard.
→ "Every voice in the room, heard."
→ "Your team's language, our job."
Precise
Numbers, not adjectives. Concrete and earned.
→ "3 action items. Assigned."
→ "Hindi + English detected live."
Inclusive
India's multilingual reality is a feature, not an edge case.
→ "हिंदी, English, Tamil — your language."
→ "Built for the way India meets."
Confident
Assured, not arrogant. Product speaks; copy supports quietly.
→ "Try it free." (not "🚀 GAME CHANGING")
→ "Your brief is ready."

"Detects Hindi, Tamil, and 9 more Indian languages."

EchoBrief · Warm Intelligence · Light Mode

BG: #FAF7F2 parchment — warm cream, NOT pure white or cold grey. Cards are #FFFFFF floating above parchment. Sidebar #F4EFE7.

Ember: #D93F0B (deeper than dark for contrast on light). Gold: #D4900A. Text: #1C1612 primary → #3D3028 → #7A6A58 muted → #B0A090 faint.

Borders: rgba(120, 90, 60, x) warm brown — NOT grey. 7% subtle, 12% soft, 20% medium.

Shadows: rgba(120,80,40,x) warm brown. Cards: shadow-card. Ember buttons: warm red glow.

Key rule: Everything warm-toned. No cold blue-grey anywhere. Parchment + ink + ember world.
